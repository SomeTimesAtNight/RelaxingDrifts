Curse of Darkness

A post-apocalyptic survival adventure awaits you.

About the Game

Curse of Darkness is a tense survival game set in a post-apocalyptic world, where the player must piece together a wrecked car to survive while avoiding deadly monsters. Four different creatures roam the world, each with unique abilities that keep you on the edge of your seat.

  • Search for car parts and gather resources.
  • Monitor your health, stamina, and noise levels to avoid monsters.
  • Multiple paths and endings based on your decisions.

Game Mechanics

Explore the unique features of Curse of Darkness:

  • **Stealth**: Sneak past monsters using the environment and reduce noise levels.
  • **Dynamic AI**: Monsters adapt to your behavior, ensuring every encounter is unique.
